  General Principles All rates are shown as $USD-pegged for convenience/realism. I look forward to a future when I can fix my rates in satoshis.
In order of descending preference, I accept payment in the following currencies:
BTC > DAI > USD > DOGE, ETH, ETC, XMR, ZEC, ARR > CAD
In general I will only accept other cryptocurrency and tokens as an additional "bonus" to base payments.
For fiat currency transfer, my descending preferences are:
Cash > Mailed Cheque > Bank Transfer > Preloaded Cards > Paypal or other private transfer
